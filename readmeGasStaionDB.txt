1.0  Information

The database contains information on the sale of gas, air-water, car wash products at a gas station for a day(2023-10-19).

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


2.0  Data Tables

The database is comprised of the following tables:

	gas_station - Name and address of gas station
	product - All products available for sale at the gas station
	sale - All sale made for the day at the gas station
	pump_gas - pumps available for the sale of gas products
	air_water - pumps available for sale of air water products
	car_wash = car wash available for sale of car wash products
